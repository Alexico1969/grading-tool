Instructions to export data from BlackBaud

Export Groups

  • BLACKBAUD: Go to the Gradebook
  • Click on "Grades by student"
  • Click on "Export data to Excel"
  • Save the file, rename to group name

Export Grades

  • PR.STEM: Go to "Grades"
  • Filter Assignments (f.e. all 4.8 assignm.)
  • Dropdown: Actions --> Export current Gradebook view
